About CICM

The CICM Missionaries officially named as the Congregation of the Immaculate Heart of Mary abbreviated C.I.C.M, is a Catholic clerical religious congregation of Pontifical Right for men established in 1862 by the Belgian Catholic priest Theophile Verbist.
